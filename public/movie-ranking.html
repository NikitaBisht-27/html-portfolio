The Best Movies According to Nikita

My top three movies of all time

Twelth Fail

This is the motivational story based on true events of a UPSC aspirant who failed his intermediate exam.

Sita Ramam

This is a romantic movie of a soldier and a princess. The movie is full of innocence and shows a beautiful depiction of love

Shershah

The story of a Kargil War Hero, Captain Vikram Batra.
